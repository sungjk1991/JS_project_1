Turks & Caicos

The Ideal Vacation Spot

The Turks and Caicos Islands are an archipelago of 40 low-lying coral islands in the Atlantic Ocean, a British Overseas Territory southeast of the Bahamas. The gateway island of Providenciales, known as Provo, is home to expansive Grace Bay Beach, with luxury resorts, shops and restaurants. Activities

Snorkeling

Dive into crystal-clear waters and explore vibrant coral reefs teeming with colorful fish and marine life. Grace Bay's Bight Reef and Smith's Reef are snorkeler's havens!

Scuba Diving

Plunge into the deep blue and experience world-class scuba diving in the Turks and Caicos Islands. Encounter shipwrecks, caves, and breathtaking wall dives that cater to divers of all skill levels.

Horseback Riding on the Beach

Gallop along the shorelines of Turks and Caicos for a picturesque and romantic experience. Sunset rides are especially magical.
